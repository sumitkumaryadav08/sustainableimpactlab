About me

I am a doctoral researcher studying how artificial intelligence is shaping energy transitions across diverse geographies.

I am based at the Monash Sustainable Development Institute, Monash University. My PhD research examines the role of artificial intelligence in energy transitions, with a focus on innovation directionality, geography of innovation, actor configurations, and socio-technical change.

My work combines sustainability transitions, innovation studies, patent analytics, and public policy perspectives to understand how emerging technologies influence low-carbon transition pathways. I am particularly interested in the “Twin Transition” at the intersection of digitalisation and sustainability.

Beyond my PhD, my broader professional experience spans sustainability, public policy, evidence reviews, research operations, digital health, innovation policy, and development research.

Sustainable Impact Lab is the personal research website of Sumit Kumar, featuring academic work, publications, projects, and reflections on AI, innovation, and sustainability transitions.
